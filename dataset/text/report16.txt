Jessica Lee, 30-year-old female experiencing severe shortness of breath and wheezing after exposure to pollen while hiking. She has a known history of asthma. Medications include albuterol inhaler and fluticasone. No known allergies. Vitals en route: BP 135/85, HR 110, RR 28 with audible wheezing, SpO2 88% on room air, improved to 95% after treatment. We administered 2.5 mg albuterol via nebulizer and provided oxygen at 6 liters per minute via mask. Her breathing has improved during transport.

Patient Care Report 28: Jacob Williams, 8-year-old male found submerged in a swimming pool, estimated underwater time of two minutes before rescue. Upon arrival, he was unresponsive but breathing spontaneously. No significant medical history; no medications or allergies. Vitals en route: BP 100/60, HR 110, RR 24, SpO2 95% with supplemental oxygen.

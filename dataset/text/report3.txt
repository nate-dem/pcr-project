Robert Thompson, 65-year-old male presenting with shortness of breath and wheezing after exposure to smoke from a house fire. History of COPD and chronic bronchitis. Medications include albuterol and tiotropium inhalers. Allergic to sulfa drugs. Vitals en route: BP 140/85, HR 110, RR 26 with labored breathing, SpO2 88% on room air, improved to 93% with oxygen. We administered albuterol nebulizer treatment and provided high-flow oxygen via non-rebreather mask at 15 liters per minute.
